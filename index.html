Frequently Asked Questions

Connectivity

Q: How do I connect via USB MIDI?

A: Simply connect your keyboard using the appropriate USB cable. The app will automatically detect compatible MIDI devices.

Q: How do I connect via Bluetooth MIDI?

A: Enable Bluetooth on your device, then select your keyboard from the Bluetooth MIDI devices list in the Settings.

Voice Management

Q: What is the MSB-LSB-PC# format?

A: This format represents Bank Select MSB, Bank Select LSB, and Program Change numbers. Example: 48-1-1

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. The app will create a backup file that you can save.
